The 7-Stage Pipeline
1
Identify
Find a new agency to target. Log their name, area/district, city, contact person, and phone number. Set the lead temperature: Hot (ready now), Warm (interested but not urgent), or Cold (unknown/untested).
✅ Move to Stage 2 once the agency is fully logged with contact details.
2
Qualify
Confirm the agency has at least 1 licensed broker. Log the total number of agents and how many hold a RERA broker license. This sets your registration target — your goal is to register the maximum number of licensed brokers on CMP, not just one.
✅ Any agency with even 1 broker qualifies. Move to Stage 3 once counts are confirmed.
3
Outreach
Make first contact — walk-in visit, phone call, or WhatsApp. Introduce CMP and explain the value: more listings, better exposure, free to register. Log the method used, what they said, and their initial reaction in the Remarks field the same day.
✅ Move to Stage 4 once a demo or meeting is agreed.
4
Demo
Present the CMP platform in person or over a screen share. Walk through listing, search, and lead management features. Have the app open and ready before entering. Know their questions in advance. Log the demo date.
✅ Move to Stage 5 once they agree to register at least one broker.
5
Register
Start registrations. Begin with the owner or senior broker, then work through every licensed broker. Track the registration count as a ratio (e.g. 7 / 42). Update the number in the card as each broker signs up. The progress bar turns green when ≥ 70% are registered.
✅ Move to Stage 6 once initial registrations are done. Keep pushing for more.
6
Follow Up
Send a WhatsApp or email within 48 hours of each visit. Use follow-ups to push for more broker registrations — remind them of brokers who haven't signed up yet. Log every follow-up in the Visit Log with the date and outcome.
✅ Move to Stage 7 once the agency is actively listing on CMP.
7
Report
Close the loop. Mark whether a listing has been submitted. Update the final registration count. Set priority to Low if fully onboarded and stable. Add any final notes. This agency is now active on CMP — check in monthly.
✅ Agency is live. Check monthly to ensure brokers remain active.
Conditions & Rules
🔒 Agency Assignment Lock
Once a rep assigns an agency to themselves in the Broker List tab, that agency is permanently locked to them. The other rep cannot claim it, even if the first rep has not yet contacted them.

This prevents overlap and ensures every agency has a clear owner. If there is a dispute over an agency, it must be resolved with the founder (Muhannad) — not by overriding the lock in the system.
🏢 Qualifying an Agency
Any agency with at least 1 RERA-licensed broker qualifies to be approached and registered on CMP. There is no minimum broker count requirement.

However, priority should be given to agencies with more licensed brokers, as the goal is to maximise the total number of registered brokers on the platform. A small agency with 2 brokers is valid, but a large one with 40 brokers should be prioritised.
📊 Registration Tracking
Registration is tracked as a ratio (registered / total licensed brokers), not as a single name.

• Green (≥ 70%) — Strong. Most of the agency is on CMP.
• Amber (40–69%) — In progress. Keep pushing.
• Red (< 40%) — Needs attention. Follow up immediately.

Update the registered count in the agency card each time a new broker signs up. Do not wait until all brokers are done.
🌡️ Trust Levels (Lead Temperature)
Hot — Agency is interested, ready to act. Visit within 24 hours. Prioritise above everything else.
Warm — Agency is interested but needs time or has conditions. Follow up within 3 days. Nurture the relationship.
Cold — Unknown or unresponsive. No expressed interest yet. Approach within the week during normal rounds.

Trust level can be upgraded as the relationship develops. A Cold agency that shows interest after a call should immediately be moved to Warm or Hot.
📝 Remarks Fields
Each agency card has two separate remarks fields:

Remarks from Agency — Write exactly what the agency contact said. Objections, questions, requests, positive signals. Verbatim quotes are useful here.
Internal Notes — Your own observations, next steps, or context not shared with the agency. This is private to the CMP team.
📅 Visit Log Rules
Every visit, call, or WhatsApp exchange must be logged in the Visit Log the same day it happens.

Log the agency name, date, method of contact, outcome, whether any broker registered, and your remarks. This shared log prevents duplicate visits and shows management a real-time view of team activity.
➕ Adding Agencies Not in the RERA List
The Broker List tab shows the top 100 agencies from the official RERA registry (out of 9,949 total). If you find an agency that is not in this list, use the + Add Agency Not in This List button at the bottom of the Broker List tab.

These custom-added agencies are still subject to the same assignment lock rule — once added and claimed, the other rep cannot select it.
🚫 What Not to Do
• Do not contact an agency already assigned to the other rep — check the Broker List first.
• Do not skip logging a visit — even a phone call that went to voicemail should be logged.
• Do not move an agency forward in the pipeline without a clear reason — each stage means something specific (see above).
• Do not share client broker details outside CMP or with third parties.
• Do not mark listing submitted unless the broker has actually listed a property on CMP.
Golden Rules
⚡
Log the same day
Every visit, call, or follow-up must be logged in the Visit Log before the end of the working day. Memory fades — log it immediately.
🎯
Maximise registrations per agency
The goal is not just to get one person signed up — it is to register every licensed broker in the agency. Start with the owner, then work through the team. Track the ratio.
⏱️
48-hour follow-up rule
After every meeting or demo, send a follow-up within 48 hours. This keeps momentum and shows professionalism. Use it to push for more broker sign-ups.
🔥
Prioritise by temperature
Hot leads within 24 hrs. Warm leads within 3 days. Cold leads within the week. Never let a Hot lead go cold because of poor follow-up timing.
🔐
Respect the assignment lock
If an agency is claimed by another rep, do not contact them. The lock exists to prevent confusion, conflicts, and wasted effort. Check before you approach.
🛡️
Protect broker data
Broker information (names, phone numbers, listings) collected through CMP is confidential. Never share it outside the CMP platform or with competing parties.
